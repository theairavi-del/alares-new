Structural and Seismic Design

 Why Choose Alares?

  • Customized Solutions: We tailor our services to meet the unique requirements of each project, ensuring optimal outcomes.
  • Commitment to Safety: We prioritize the safety of occupants and the public by      adhering to stringent design standards and best practices.

At Alares, we specialize in delivering comprehensive Structural and Seismic Design Services that ensure the safety, resilience, and longevity of your structures. Our team of experienced engineers is dedicated to creating innovative solutions that meet the highest standards of structural integrity and seismic performance.

We offer a full spectrum of structural engineering services tailored to your project's specific needs:

  • New Construction Design: Developing efficient and robust structural systems      for new buildings and infrastructure.
  • Structural  Assessments: Conducting thorough evaluations of existing structures to      determine their condition and capacity.
  • Rehabilitation and Retrofit Design: Designing solutions to strengthen and extend the life of aging or damaged structures.
  • Foundation  Engineering: Providing expert design for various foundation systems to ensure stability and support.
  • Construction Support Services: Offering on-site inspections, quality assurance, and  project management to facilitate seamless execution.

Our Seismic Design Services

Understanding the critical importance of seismic resilience, we provide specialized services to safeguard structures against earthquake hazards:

  • Seismic Risk Assessments: Evaluating potential seismic vulnerabilities and their      implications for your structures.
  • Performance-Based Seismic Design: Implementing advanced design methodologies to achieve desired seismic performance levels.
  • Seismic Retrofit Solutions: Upgrading existing structures to enhance their      earthquake resistance and comply with current codes.
  • Compliance with Seismic Codes and Standards: Ensuring all designs meet or exceed the latest local and international seismic regulations.
