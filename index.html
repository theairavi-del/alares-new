Our Story

Building excellence since 2007

A Legacy of Excellence

Since 2007, Alares has been providing design services to clients across the nation. Our team of Architects and Engineers shares a genuine drive to find the best solutions for every project.

Our approach reflects the leadership of our founder, Don Maggioli — a West Point graduate and former Green Beret officer. This military "can-do" attitude guides how we tackle challenges.

The name Alares comes from the Roman militia, known for their swiftness in combat. We achieve our clients' objectives swiftly, without sacrificing quality for speed.
