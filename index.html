Our Story

Engineering excellence built on integrity

Founded by West Point graduate and Green Beret officer Don Maggioli, Alares brings military precision and leadership to every project. The name Alares comes from the Roman militia — swift, precise, effective.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional design and engineering solutions that enhance patient care, improve efficiency, and stand the test of time. We achieve our clients' objectives swiftly, without sacrificing quality for speed.

Our Approach

Our military "can-do" attitude guides how we tackle challenges: with the energy to complete the best job possible and to always leave the world better than when we found it. Precision in every detail.
