Our Story

A Legacy of Excellence

Since 2007, Alares has been providing design services to clients across the nation. Our team of Architects and Engineers shares a genuine and collective drive to find the best solutions for every project.

Our approach reflects the leadership of our founder, Don Maggioli — a West Point graduate and former Green Beret officer. This military "can-do" attitude guides how we tackle challenges.

Our Name

The name Alares was selected because our philosophy is to always achieve our clients' objectives in a swift manner, without sacrificing quality for speed.

Alares were a kind of militia among the Romans, so called from ala — Latin for "wing" — because of their lightness and swiftness in combat.
